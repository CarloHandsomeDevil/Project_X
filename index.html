About Us

Tamaraw Beer is more than just a brewery; it's a dedication to craftsmanship and tradition. Founded with a vision to deliver premium quality brews, we strive to offer a diverse range of beers that cater to every palate. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in each sip of our meticulously crafted beers, which embody the rich heritage and passion of our founders.

Founded in 2024, Tamaraw Beer began as a dream of Carlo Dulutan, a visionary entrepreneur with a deep-rooted passion for brewing. His journey started with a humble desire to create beers that not only satisfy but also elevate the drinking experience. Today, Tamaraw Beer stands as a testament to his dedication and the collective effort of our team.

At Tamaraw Beer, our goal is simple yet profound: to redefine the art of brewing. We aim to expand our reach while maintaining our unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Whether you're enjoying our beers at one of our locations, exploring our seasonal offerings, or connecting with us through our community engagements, we invite you to experience the essence of Tamaraw Beer.

Immerse yourself in the world of Tamaraw Beer, where tradition meets innovation to craft exceptional brews. Born from a legacy of quality and passion, each sip of Tamaraw Beer embodies a celebration of finely brewed perfection. From crisp lagers to robust ales, our diverse selection caters to every palate, promising a journey of flavor and satisfaction. Whether enjoyed at our welcoming locations, delivered to your doorstep, or featured in our seasonal promotions, Tamaraw Beer invites you to savor the true essence of premium brewing excellence.
